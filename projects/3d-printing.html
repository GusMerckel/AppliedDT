Getting started with 3D printing

Key points

  • Understand FDM (Fused Deposition Modelling) — layer-by-layer extrusion
  • Design for printing: overhangs, supports, layer orientation, tolerance
  • Use slicing software (Cura, PrusaSlicer) to prepare your file
  • Connects to A4.1 Manufacturing techniques and B4.1 Production systems
