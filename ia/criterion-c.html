Criterion C — Prototype Development

Criterion C is where you actually make your prototype. You need to document the making process, show evidence of skill, and demonstrate how you resolved problems as they arose. Maximum 6 marks.

What

What the examiner is looking for

Key points

  • Clear evidence of making — photographs, videos, annotated process shots
  • Demonstration of relevant technical skills
  • Evidence of problem-solving during making
  • A functional prototype that can be tested
