Criterion E — Impact & Sustainability

Criterion E asks you to consider the broader impact of your design — on users, society, and the environment. This is where your understanding of design in context really matters. Maximum 2 marks.

On this page

What

What the examiner is looking for

Key points

  • Consideration of social, cultural, and environmental impacts
  • Connection to sustainability frameworks (TBL, circular economy, LCA)
  • Honest reflection — both positive and negative impacts
  • Suggestions for how the design could be made more sustainable
