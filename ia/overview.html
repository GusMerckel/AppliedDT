Overview & Requirements

The Internal Assessment is a design project worth 30 marks. You will identify a real problem, develop a solution, build a prototype, and evaluate your work against your design specification.

What is the IA?

Key points

  • 30 marks total — submitted digitally
  • Approx. 40–50 hours of work
  • A real problem for a real client or user
  • Presented as a design portfolio (no page limit — but quality over quantity)
