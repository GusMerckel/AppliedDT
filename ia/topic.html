Choosing your IA Topic

Choosing the right topic is one of the most important decisions in your IA. A strong topic has a clear problem, an identifiable user, and genuine room for design innovation.

On this page

What

What makes a good IA topic?

Key points

  • Solves a real problem for a specific person or group
  • Has a clear design gap — not just a copy of something existing
  • Can be prototyped within your available tools and time
  • Connects to curriculum topics (materials, ergonomics, systems…)
  • You are genuinely interested in it

Common

Common mistakes to avoid

Key points

  • Topics that are too broad ('a better chair')
  • Topics with no real client or user to consult
  • Purely software solutions with no physical prototype
  • Copying a commercial product with only minor changes
